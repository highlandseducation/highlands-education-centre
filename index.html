About Our School

Welcome to Highlands Education Centre, a mixed day private primary school located in the heart of Eldoret, Maili Nne (Kambi Thomas area), Uasin Gishu County.

We are dedicated to providing quality, child-centered education in a safe, supportive, and inspiring environment. Our experienced teachers focus on academic excellence, moral values, creativity, and holistic development.

Join our family and give your child the best foundation for success!
